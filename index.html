nesflix-web

animated gif → playable .nes rom, in the browser. a port of NESFlix by NO CARRIER and Batsly Adams.

input requirements

  • animated GIF, exactly 128 × 128 px
  • up to 64 frames (MMC3 mapper limit)
  • exactly 4 colors, paletted
  • see Levi's Digital Voyage tutorial for GIF prep instructions using EZGIF.com
